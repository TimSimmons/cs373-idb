      Player_Year

Contains player statistics for a given year.

The Player_Year model holds information about a player which is likely to change from year to year. For example, any yearly statistics such as batting average or the team the player played for that specific year would be attributes of this model.

The Player_Year model has a many to one relationship with the Team_Year, Year, and Player models.
